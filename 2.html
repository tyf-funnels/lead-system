What We Do

Craft at every
scale.

From a single room to an entire estate — Forma's disciplines span architecture, interiors, landscape, and bespoke furniture design.

I

Residential Architecture

New builds, extensions, and full restorations for private residences of distinction. We work at every scale, from the intimate to the monumental.

II

Interior Architecture

Complete interior architecture and spatial design — materials, light, proportion, and the precise choreography of how a room is lived in.

III

Commercial & Hospitality

Flagship retail, private members' clubs, boutique hotels, and executive spaces. Environments that articulate a brand without stating it.

IV

Landscape Design

The space between walls matters as much as the space within them. Forma's landscape practice extends architecture into terrain and season.

V

Bespoke Furniture & Objects

One-of-a-kind pieces designed and fabricated in collaboration with master craftspeople. Every object carries the geometry of the space it was made for.

VI

Project Management

Full owner's representation from brief to handover. We act as your single point of accountability across every contractor, consultant, and timeline.

Our Method

A process as considered
as the result.

01

The Briefing

We begin with deep listening — your life, your aesthetic instincts, the way you move through space. No forms, no questionnaires. A conversation.

02

Concept Development

From the brief we build a spatial narrative — a vision that guides every decision to come. Materials, light, proportion, sequence.

03

Design Development

The concept is refined into precise technical drawings, material specifications, and a construction-ready set of documents your builders can trust.

04

Build & Handover

We stay on site. Forma manages construction to the same standard we hold our drawings — obsessive, uncompromising, and entirely accountable.
